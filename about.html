About Me

Iverson Ruffin

Artist & Web Designer

Hello, I'm Iverson Ruffin(hello, again, if you are coming from the home page). I am a Fall 2023 graduate from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.
I am an aspiring illustrator that enjoys learning, as well as teaching others about computer science or drawing. Currently, I am looking for work in a creative industry that combines art and technology; a job that comes with fun in the description and challenges me and my skillset.
At some point, later in my career, I would love to go back to the classroom to help guide others with the experiences I have gathered throughout my journey - whether that is in computer science, art, or some other field I have knowledge on.
I'm not sure where my life will take me, but I am here for the ride.
